About Rebel Table

Rebel Table was born in Oxford with a simple idea — good food should feel like home. Our kitchen blends classic Southern comfort with modern flavor, using fresh, local ingredients and a whole lot of heart.

From weekday lunches to weekend dinners, we believe in slowing down, sharing stories, and serving meals that bring people together. Because here, every table is a Rebel Table.
